# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community.

Be considerate, give constructive feedback, respect privacy, and focus discussion on the work. Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, and publication of another person's private information are unacceptable.

Project maintainers may remove content or participation that violates these expectations. Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner. Maintainers will review reports fairly and protect reporter privacy where possible.
